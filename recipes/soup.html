Absolutely Ultimate Potato Soup

Description

I have made this potato soup for many whom have given it this title. It takes a bit of effort, but it's well worth it.

This potato soup comes together quickly with basic ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Potatoes
  • Chicken Stock
  • Butter
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Heavy Cream
  • Dried Tarragon
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the Bacon
  2. Cook the Soup
  3. Stir cream into Soup
  4. Blend and Serve
